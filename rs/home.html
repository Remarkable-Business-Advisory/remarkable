Where Should You Register Your Business in Dubai?

Dubai is a popular destination for entrepreneurs looking to start a business in the Middle East

Establishing a business in Dubai can be overwhelming. Business Setup Consultants can help by deciding on which company type best suits your business plan and choose to register under a free zone license, mainland license, or offshore license.

Mainland Company Formation
  • Foreigners can have 100% ownership in Dubai Mainland
  • Businesses may be conducted effortlessly from anywhere in the UAE
  • Ability to apply for UAE government contracts
Free Zone Company Formation
  • Get your trade license without visiting UAE
  • No physical office is required for free zone license in UAE
  • Exemptions from all taxes on the whole business
Offshore Company Formation
  • Setup an offshore company in Dubai with no minimum capital requirements
  • Common laws along with No Limits on capital repatriation
  • Availability of a wide range of legal structures
